Real Estate Consultancy

Expert advisory for property investment and development

We provide specialized real estate consultancy services, offering expert guidance on property investment, development, and market positioning throughout the UAE.

1. Real Estate Research & Market Studies

  • Market analysis covering supply, demand, and pricing trends for residential, commercial, hospitality, and industrial sectors
  • Competitor and area analysis for investment decision-making
  • Feasibility studies evaluating financial performance (ROI, IRR, Payback period)
  • Highest and Best Use analysis for land and property
  • Quarterly and annual reports on real estate performance and market forecasts

2. Real Estate Valuation

  • Property and land valuation for sale, purchase, or financing purposes
  • Valuation reports in accordance with international standards (RICS, IVS)
  • Fair market value estimation of real estate assets
  • Portfolio valuation for investors, developers, and financial institutions

3. Development Advisory

  • Project concept and planning consultancy
  • Feasibility and design studies for new developments
  • Recommendations on the optimal property type (residential, commercial, mixed-use)
  • Pricing and sales strategy development

4. Investment Advisory

  • Identification of local and international investment opportunities
  • Financial risk and return analysis for real estate projects
  • Investment diversification strategy and portfolio management
  • Support in mergers, acquisitions, and asset disposal processes

5. Sales & Marketing Advisory

  • Development of comprehensive marketing strategies for new projects
  • Defining target segments and promotional tactics
  • Project launch planning and campaign management
  • Performance evaluation and enhancement of sales teams

6. Legal & Regulatory Advisory

  • Reviewing and drafting real estate contracts and agreements
  • Ensuring compliance with local laws and property regulations
  • Advising on licensing, registration, and documentation procedures
  • Support in real estate dispute resolution and arbitration

7. Project Management Consultancy (PMC)

  • Supervision of project development from design to delivery
  • Coordination between contractors, consultants, and developers
  • Cost control, timeline management, and quality assurance

8. International Real Estate Advisory

  • Assisting clients in overseas property investment and acquisitions
  • Comparative analysis of international real estate markets
  • Partnering with global agencies for cross-border opportunities

9. Strategic Reporting & Recommendations

  • Preparation of executive reports for investors and management
  • Trend analysis and future market projections
  • Data-driven recommendations for strategic decision-making

Why Choose Us

Our consultancy team brings deep market knowledge and analytical expertise to help you make informed real estate decisions. We provide objective, data-driven insights that maximize returns and minimize risks.
