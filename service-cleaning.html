Cleaning Services

Professional cleaning solutions for every space

Our professional cleaning services maintain pristine environments for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. We use eco-friendly products and proven techniques to deliver exceptional results.

1. Indoor Cleaning Services

  • Office and administrative area cleaning: dusting surfaces, cleaning desks, and sanitizing switches and handles
  • Restroom and public area cleaning: disinfecting toilets and sinks, refilling hygiene supplies, and removing odors
  • Floor cleaning: sweeping, scrubbing, polishing, and stain removal for marble, ceramic, wood, or vinyl floors
  • Carpet and upholstery cleaning: deep steam cleaning, stain and odor removal
  • Glass and window cleaning (interior): cleaning and polishing internal glass surfaces for maximum transparency

2. Outdoor Cleaning Services

  • Facade and glass cleaning: using rope access systems or hydraulic lifts, applying specialized detergents to prevent dirt buildup
  • Exterior wall cleaning: removing dust, grease, and mold from stone or concrete walls with high-pressure water
  • Entrance, walkway, and rooftop cleaning: sweeping and washing external floors and surfaces
  • Parking area cleaning: removing oil stains, washing concrete floors and walls, collecting litter and debris

3. Environmental and Hygiene Care

  • Building disinfection: periodic sanitization using approved disinfectants for offices, halls, and public spaces
  • Pest and rodent control: routine inspection and application of safe, eco-friendly pest management methods
  • Waste management: segregating recyclable and general waste, ensuring safe and sustainable disposal

4. Specialized Cleaning Services

  • Post-construction or renovation cleaning
  • High-rise and complex architectural glass cleaning
  • Water tank cleaning (underground and rooftop)
  • HVAC duct and vent cleaning

5. Operational Standards and Quality Assurance

  • Using professional cleaning machines and modern equipment (steam, pressure washers, etc.)
  • Providing staff training on safety and cleaning techniques
  • Following fixed cleaning schedules and performance plans
  • Implementing quality control and performance reporting systems

Why Choose Us

Our trained cleaning professionals deliver consistent, high-quality results using industry-leading equipment and eco-friendly products. We customize our services to meet your specific needs and schedule.
