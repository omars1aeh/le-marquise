Facilities Management

Complete end-to-end facilities management solutions

La Marquise Facilities Management offers integrated facilities management services designed to optimize your operations and maintain your assets at peak performance.

1. Operations and Maintenance

  • Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing (MEP) Services: Maintenance of HVAC systems, elevators, lighting, power supply, and water networks
  • Preventive and Corrective Maintenance: Regular inspections and scheduled servicing to avoid breakdowns, plus emergency repair works
  • Building Management Systems (BMS): Monitoring and control of energy use, lighting, HVAC performance, and equipment alerts

2. Soft Services

  • Cleaning & Housekeeping: Daily cleaning of offices, public areas, and glass fa√ßades, plus hospitality services
  • Security Services: Access control, CCTV monitoring, and on-site guarding
  • Waste Management: Waste segregation, recycling, and safe disposal of medical or industrial waste
  • Landscaping & Grounds Maintenance: Garden design, maintenance, and irrigation system management

3. Energy and Sustainability Management

  • Monitoring energy and water consumption
  • Implementing efficiency solutions to reduce waste and operating costs
  • Supporting green building initiatives (LEED / Estidama certification)

4. Contract and Vendor Management

  • Negotiation and administration of supplier and maintenance contracts
  • Vendor performance evaluation and compliance monitoring
  • Ensuring adherence to quality, safety, and service standards

5. Asset Management

  • Creating and maintaining a detailed Asset Register
  • Monitoring each asset's life cycle and maintenance history
  • Planning for asset renewal, upgrades, and capital replacement

6. Financial and Operational Management

  • Preparing operating and maintenance budgets
  • Monthly and annual performance reporting to clients
  • Managing tenant or occupant requests and complaints

7. Support and Administrative Services

  • Reception and front-desk management
  • Parking management
  • Logistics and internal relocation (furniture, equipment, etc.)

8. Digital Transformation and Smart FM Solutions

  • Use of Computer-Aided Facilities Management (CAFM / CMMS) systems
  • Real-time dashboards and performance analytics
  • Mobile apps for tenant service requests and reporting

Why Choose Us

Our experienced team delivers reliable, cost-effective facilities management that allows you to focus on your core business. We use the latest technology and best practices to ensure your facilities operate efficiently 24/7.
