About Haven House

Haven House General Contracting is the construction arm of La Marquise Group, specializing in delivering high-quality construction and finishing solutions for residential and commercial projects across the UAE.

With a reputation built on precision, reliability, and craftsmanship, we manage projects from concept to completion, ensuring every build meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and client satisfaction.

Our experienced team of architects, engineers, and construction professionals work collaboratively to bring architectural visions to life, whether it's a luxury villa, commercial complex, or large-scale renovation project.

We pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, within budget, and exceeding expectations through meticulous planning, skilled execution, and unwavering commitment to excellence.
