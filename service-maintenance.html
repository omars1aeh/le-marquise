Maintenance Services

Reliable maintenance and repair solutions for all properties

We provide comprehensive maintenance and repair services for residential and commercial properties. Our skilled technicians handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs.

1. Electrical Maintenance

  • Inspection and maintenance of electrical panels and equipment
  • Repair and replacement of lighting fixtures (indoor and outdoor)
  • Periodic testing of electrical systems to prevent malfunctions
  • Replacement of switches, sockets, and damaged wiring
  • Load testing and ensuring compliance with safety standards

2. Plumbing & Water Systems Maintenance

  • Repair of leaks in pipes, valves, and fittings
  • Cleaning and maintenance of underground and rooftop water tanks
  • Inspection and servicing of water pumps and pressure systems
  • Maintenance of bathrooms, kitchens, and drainage systems
  • Installation or upgrading of sanitary and wastewater systems

3. HVAC (Heating, Ventilation & Air Conditioning) Maintenance

  • Servicing and repair of split and central air conditioning units
  • Cleaning of filters, coils, and ducts
  • Checking and refilling refrigerant gas levels
  • Ensuring efficient air circulation and cooling performance
  • Implementing preventive maintenance schedules

4. Mechanical & Structural Maintenance

  • Repairing doors, windows, locks, and hinges
  • Carpentry and metalwork repairs
  • Maintenance of roofs, structures, and ceilings
  • Crack and corrosion treatment for walls and structural components

5. Painting & Finishing Works

  • Interior and exterior wall painting
  • Crack repair and repainting as required
  • Moisture and mold treatment
  • Minor decorative and finishing repair works

6. Cleaning & Housekeeping Support

  • Post-maintenance deep cleaning
  • Removal of debris and waste after repair works
  • Polishing of surfaces and floors
  • General upkeep to improve overall appearance

7. Roof & Insulation Maintenance

  • Inspection and repair of roof leaks
  • Maintenance of waterproofing and thermal insulation systems
  • Cleaning and unclogging of rainwater drainage systems

8. Safety & Security Systems Maintenance

  • Inspection and servicing of fire alarm and sprinkler systems
  • Maintenance of CCTV cameras and access control systems
  • Ensuring emergency lighting and exit signage are functional

9. Preventive & Scheduled Maintenance

  • Preparation of monthly, quarterly, and annual maintenance plans
  • Documentation and performance reporting of all maintenance works
  • Use of Computerized Maintenance Management Systems (CMMS)
  • Regular inspections to minimize breakdowns and downtime

10. Technical Support & Emergency Maintenance

  • 24/7 emergency maintenance services
  • Immediate response to power failures, leaks, and breakdowns
  • Deployment of specialized technicians for urgent issues

Why Choose Us

Our experienced maintenance team responds quickly to your needs with professional, high-quality workmanship. We use quality materials and proven techniques to ensure lasting repairs and customer satisfaction.
