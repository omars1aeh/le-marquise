About Al Dahan Al Masi

Al Dahan Al Masi General Maintenance is a specialized maintenance services company within the La Marquise Group family, providing comprehensive technical support and maintenance solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial facilities across the UAE.

With a team of highly skilled technicians and engineers, we deliver prompt, reliable maintenance services covering electrical, plumbing, HVAC, mechanical systems, and general building maintenance.

Our commitment to quality workmanship, rapid response times, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for property owners, facility managers, and businesses seeking dependable maintenance solutions.

We combine technical expertise with modern tools and equipment to ensure every maintenance task is completed efficiently, safely, and to the highest standards.
