General Contracting Services

Quality construction and finishing solutions for all projects

We provide comprehensive general contracting services for construction, finishing, and renovation projects. Our experienced team delivers quality workmanship, professional project management, and reliable execution for residential and commercial developments.

1. Core Construction Works

  • Excavation, backfilling, and site leveling
  • Construction of concrete foundations, footings, and slabs
  • Execution of columns, beams, walls, and ceilings
  • Blockwork and masonry construction
  • Waterproofing and thermal insulation of roofs and foundations

2. Finishing Works

  • Internal and external plastering works
  • Painting of walls and ceilings with high-quality materials
  • Installation of floor finishes (ceramic, porcelain, parquet, marble, etc.)
  • Gypsum board and false ceiling works
  • Aluminum, glass, and wooden door installations

3. Electrical and Plumbing Works (MEP)

  • Installation of electrical networks and power systems
  • Lighting systems and electrical fixture installations
  • Plumbing and sanitary system installation and testing
  • Fixing bathroom and kitchen fittings
  • Ensuring compliance with safety and technical standards

4. HVAC Works

  • Installation of split and central air conditioning systems
  • Ducting and piping works for ventilation systems
  • Inspection and maintenance for efficient performance

5. Simple Landscaping Works

  • Leveling and preparation of garden areas
  • Planting grass, trees, and decorative plants
  • Installation of interlock tiles and simple irrigation systems

6. General Building Maintenance

  • Electrical, plumbing, and AC maintenance services
  • Repair of cracks, painting, and minor construction works
  • Replacement of broken tiles, windows, and doors
  • Post-maintenance cleaning and site handover

7. Project Management

  • Preparing project schedules and work programs
  • Managing manpower, materials, and subcontractors
  • Monitoring quality, safety, and cost control
  • Providing progress reports and documentation

8. Procurement and Supply

  • Purchasing and supplying construction materials from approved vendors
  • Ensuring material quality and compliance with specifications
  • Proper storage and inventory management at site

9. Health and Safety

  • Implementing safety procedures on-site
  • Providing personal protective equipment (PPE) to workers
  • Ensuring compliance with local safety regulations

10. Advisory and Consulting Services

  • Providing basic engineering and design advice
  • Recommending cost-effective construction materials and methods
  • Offering technical solutions to improve quality and reduce expenses

Why Choose Us

Our contracting team brings extensive experience in construction and project delivery. We combine quality craftsmanship with efficient project management to ensure your project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.
