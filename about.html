About La Marquise Group

A 100% UAE-based group delivering excellence in management services

Our Story

La Marquise Group for Corporate Management is a leading investment and management group based in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.

The group oversees and operates a portfolio of specialized companies in sectors including construction, maintenance, facilities management, interior design, real estate management, and real estate consultancy.

Since its establishment, the group has pursued a clear objective: to build a powerful entity that combines operational efficiency with strategic vision, providing integrated solutions that meet local and regional market needs according to the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

At La Marquise, we believe that diversity and integration are the foundation of success. That's why we established and manage a group of companies covering all aspects of the real estate and construction sectors—from planning, design, and execution to management, maintenance, investment, and consultancy—offering our clients a complete ecosystem of services under one umbrella.
